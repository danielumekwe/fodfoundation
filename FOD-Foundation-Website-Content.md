## Changing Lives, One Ability at a Time

At Focus on Disability Foundation, impact is not measured in numbers alone — it is measured in restored dignity, regained independence, and lives transformed. Across Nigeria, we work with children and adults living with disabilities and their families to achieve real choice, independence, and opportunity.

Our vision is of a transformed society where people with disabilities can live life to the full. Every wheelchair delivered, every skill taught, and every family supported brings that vision closer to reality.

In a country where there are an estimated 22 million or more people living with disabilities — and where disability remains one of the most neglected dimensions of equal rights — our work fills a gap that too often goes unaddressed. This is the difference we are making.

## The Difference We Make

### 1. Restoring Mobility & Independence

A core part of our work is relieving poverty and distress among people with physical disabilities in Nigeria through the provision of wheelchairs, mobility aids, and equipment designed to meet their needs. For a child who has crawled for years or an adult unable to leave home, a wheelchair is not just equipment — it is freedom, dignity, and a return to community life.

**Impact so far:** [XXX] wheelchairs and mobility devices delivered to individuals across [locations], enabling them to attend school, work, worship, and move freely for the first time.

### 2. Building Skills for Self-Sufficiency

We enable children and adults with disabilities to be self-sufficient and integrated into society — rather than spend a life in an institutional setting. By equipping our beneficiaries with essential life, social, and vocational skills, we help them earn a living and take control of their own futures.

**Impact so far:** [XX] beneficiaries trained in [skills — e.g. tailoring, ICT, crafts], with [XX] now running their own small enterprises or in gainful employment.

### 3. Caring for Vulnerable Children

Through the Focus on Disability Foundation Children's Home, we provide care, shelter, and a nurturing environment for vulnerable children with disabilities — developing their physical, mental, emotional, and social skills so they can reach their full potential.

**Impact so far:** [XX] children given a safe home and holistic care; [XX] supported into inclusive education.

### 4. Educating Families & Communities

We educate families and communities about the needs of children and adults with disabilities and special needs — challenging stigma, correcting harmful myths, and building acceptance so that persons with disabilities are welcomed, not hidden away.

**Impact so far:** [XX] awareness outreaches and community sensitization events reaching [X,XXX] people.

### 5. Providing Educational Materials & Disability Aids

We provide educational materials and disability aids to help beneficiaries achieve their potential, removing the practical barriers that keep children with disabilities out of the classroom.

**Impact so far:** [XXX] learners equipped with assistive learning tools and school materials.

### 6. Campaigning for Change

Working alongside partner organizations in Nigeria and internationally, we campaign for equal rights for people with disabilities and for the fuller implementation of disability-protection laws — because lasting change requires systems to change, not just individuals to be helped.
